## 📌 Description

**DmScreenPrivacy** is a Vencord plugin that automatically blurs your Direct Message (DM) conversations whenever you start screen sharing or streaming, helping keep your private messages hidden from viewers.

Need to read a message? Simply click the **toggle button in the top-right corner** of the chat to instantly reveal or blur the conversation whenever you want.

## ✨ Features

* 🔒 Automatically blurs private DM conversations during screen sharing.
* 👁️ Instantly reveal or hide the chat using the top-right toggle button.
* ⚡ Activates automatically as soon as screen sharing starts.
* 🛡️ Protects your privacy and prevents accidental exposure of personal messages.
